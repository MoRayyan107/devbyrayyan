Homepage

In this website we will discuss ways in which computing and technology is being used to support UN goal 13: take urgent action to combat climate change.

From this page you can use the nav bar under the title to click onto one of our informative web-pages where a subtopic of the overarching goal will be discused.

The subtopics we have chosen to write about are areas we feel are especialy important in reaching the goals of the UN and utilize computing in interesting ways

The topics we have chosen to discuss are as follows:

  1. satellite monitoring of the ozone layer
  2. How quantum computing can help
  3. education
  4. Effects on our ocean and how computing is helping
  5. Renewable energy
  6. simulating environmental changes

Through this website we hope to educate about the great ways computing is currently being used to help save our planet in many different ways.
