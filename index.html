About Me

Hello World!!, I'm Mohammad Rayyan Adhoni, a 20-year-old student pursuing a Master of Engineering in Computer Science at the University of Strathclyde in Glasgow. My passion for technology, which began at Gems United Indian School in Abu Dhabi, has shaped my path ever since.

I'm most passionate about the challenges of back-end engineering: architecting systems, managing data, and building the robust APIs that power modern applications. While my primary focus isn't on the front-end, I see it as an essential component of the whole, and this experience enables me to build more effective and thoughtful back-end solutions. Dedicated to continuous growth, I've enrolled in a coding bootcamp to accelerate my learning alongside my university studies.

When I'm not coding, you can find me on Instagram or playing video games.
